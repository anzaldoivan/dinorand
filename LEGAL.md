# Legal and Project Scope

DinoRand is an unofficial, fan-made interoperability project. It is not affiliated with,
authorized by, endorsed by, or sponsored by Capcom. “Dino Crisis” and related names, marks,
game code, and assets belong to Capcom Co., Ltd. and their respective owners.

The MIT license covers only original DinoRand project work. Third-party material remains under
its own license and is identified in [THIRD-PARTY-NOTICES.md](THIRD-PARTY-NOTICES.md) and in the
dependency inventory packaged with each release.

DinoRand ships no game assets, executables, or game-text tables. Users must provide their own
legally obtained copy. The supported editions are the GOG DRM-free releases; Steam/Enigma-protected
builds are unsupported. DinoRand does not bypass or circumvent a technological protection measure.

The project is intended for interoperability and personal use with games the user owns. Users are
responsible for complying with applicable law and license terms. This statement is practical
risk-reduction guidance, not legal advice; obtain advice from a qualified lawyer when needed.
